Personal Best Games

My top 5 games of all-time.

The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild

This is my favourite game. It makes me feel the joy of playing when i was a kid all over again.
Its sequel is objectively better but didn't have the same impact for me personally.

The Last of Us

A games with a great story accompanied with satisfying gameplay.

Resident Evil 4 Remake

It's simply a beautiful game graphicaly and a technically sound game like its previous remake in the series.
Gameplay-wise it is similar compared to the Resident evil 2 and 3 remake which are also great games,
with some quality of life feature and improvement, for instance knife-parrying is a nice touch.

Uncharted 4

Entertaining game with a movie-like experience.
Just sit back enjoy all the platforming, shooting, driving goodness packaged in a enjoyable story.

Subnautica

Survival game with a great gameplay in term of exploration, immersion, overall progression in this type of genre.
